## 1. Executive Summary

This quotation provides a comprehensive cost breakdown for the development, deployment, and support of the National Healthcare Management System (NHMS) across five major healthcare facilities in Equatorial Guinea.

### Target Facilities:
1. **Regional Hospital of Malabo** (250+ beds, 150-200 staff)
2. **Regional Hospital of Bata** (200+ beds, 120-150 staff)
3. **General Hospital of Sampaka** (150+ beds, 80-100 staff)
4. **Dr. Loeri Comba Polyclinic** (50 beds, 40-60 staff)
5. **New INSESO Bata** (100+ beds, 60-80 staff)

**Total Expected Users:** 500+ healthcare workers
**Total Patient Coverage:** ~2,600 patients per day across all facilities

### Project Timeline: 18 Months (Phased Implementation)
### Total Project Cost: **$1,847,000 USD** (Year 1)
